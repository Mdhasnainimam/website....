Vision Solutions 1：360° Around View Driving Assistance System

The 360° panoramic view image safety driving assistance system can realize a true 360° panoramic birds-eye view, and achieve 360° no dead angle monitoring, which helps to ensure safe driving! It is specially designed for trucks, buses, emergency vehicles, etc., easy to install and calibrate
The system uses a 190°FHD1080P wide-angle fisheye camera with a horizontal viewing angle greater than 190° and a vertical viewing angle greater than 130°
360° seamless splicing, multi-channel customization according to the actual model of the customer
Panoramic image synthesis technology, distortion correction of four channels of 1080p full HD images, seamless splicing, shaping the real scene of driving, realizing a true 360-degree panoramic birds-eye view
